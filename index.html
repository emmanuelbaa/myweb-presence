Welcome To my Page

Emmanuel Baah is a creative, problem-solving, and idea-sharing personality who has gone through his education with much dedication. He speaks four languages and he's fluent in all four. A graduate from the University of Ghana who studied Agricultural Engineering in the year 2021. The love of art and science came to him ever since he was in Senior High School and there, and even though he was a science student, he still took pens and colored pencils to sketch or draw something. This continued through his stay at the university for his undergraduate studies at the University of Ghana. Due to his artistic thinking, he is passionate and loved subjects such as engineering drawing and 3D modeling with CAD software during his undergraduate studies. He loves designing and visualizing ideas into reality, The fact that ideas can be turned into visuals actually makes him happy, and would love to do more as a creative person.
